White Paper: CarWoo! Stealth Shoppers

While at CarWoo!, we developed a focus on core values that varied by customer set. Our consumer values were focused on anonymity, privacy and security. This placed a burden on the dealer side of the business where dealerships spend millions of dollars a year discouraging anonymous car shopping.
The industry coined the term "stealth" for the shoppers who research without identifying themselves by supplying contact information in exchange for vehicle pricing. Due to the nature of our consumer product, we had to quickly develop expertise on successful dealer techniques when approaching this expanding demographic. This piece was used for dealer collatoral in support of those efforts.
